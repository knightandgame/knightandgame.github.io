About me

As a self-learned and dedicated web designer, I pride myself on my ability to blend aesthetics and functionality seamlessly. My goal is to create sophisticated digital spaces that leave a lasting impression on visitors while ensuring an intuitive and user-friendly experience. My passion for clean and professional web design drives me to push the boundaries of creativity, delivering websites that resonate with clients and users alike.

  • 2019 October

    I embarked on my first project, Kingsportal.ama.ph, a landing page for the corporate employee portal catering to all AMA Education System Inc. employees, conglomerates, sister companies, and partners. This project marked my initial exposure to HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript.

  • 2020 March

    I successfully completed another project for the Calidad Group Asia, designing their corporate front-end website. The website featured seamless link integrations, an informative about page, a contact us page with JotForm integration, services section, captivating digital posters, and a range of engaging content.

  • January 2021

    I achieved a significant milestone by securing my first external client, Amistad Spa. I proudly crafted a comprehensive corporate front-end website for them, highlighting four responsive and content-rich web pages.
